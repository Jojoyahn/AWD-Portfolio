THE WHEEL OF EQUIVALENT EXPOSURE

The Wheel of Equivalent Exposure application is a mock app designed to help new photographers create beautiful photographs. Based on the Law of Equivalent Exposure, this interactive app allows users to match corresponding f-stops and shutter speeds to maximize bracketing and to get a better understanding of how a camera works. The app also includes a Basics page where users can go to learn the basic terminology and information to get started. In addition, there is a Resources page for users to get a first hand look on how professional photographers are shooting their images. In correspondence with the app, I have designed a mini promotional site.
