BRANDING SWEATERS FOR TWO

Sweaters For Two is clothing brand based on West Virginian folk tale Sheepsquatch. The goal was to create a brand that incorporated a theme of romance and the Sheepsquatch. With this, I created a company that makes sweaters out of the fur of a Sheepsquatch. However, these aten't just any sweaters, they have two head holes are meant to be shared with a loved one.
